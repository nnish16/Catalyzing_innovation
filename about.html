Innovizer's Origin Story



Having worked in new product development for companies including PepsiCo, Nestlé, Disney, Kraft, Abbott Labs, Mattel, & Northern Trust Bank, & having also been involved in & lead brainstorms & innovation programs for Ford Motors, P&G, Shiseido, PNC Bank, Arby’s, Subway, Avon, Givaudan, the ANA, & others, I’ve watched brainstorm participants vary the same limited number of product & service features over & over, neglecting so many other possible types of innovation. Thomas Edison, a methodical inventor, famously said “to invent you need a good imagination & a pile of junk” aka stimuli. People need stimuli & a checklist, to remind them of ALL the ways there are to innovate.


Inspired by Ferran Adriá from Spain, considered by many to be the most creative chef who ever lived, I travelled the world taking pictures of anything I’d never seen before in a country or industry. I captured 1100 innovation examples, looked for patterns of innovation, & settled on 63 different types of innovation that the most successful products & services globally exhibited. The examples were turned into a book & method to inspire marketers to innovate more systematically, completely & strategically. Each example describes why the innovation was strategic… how it helped the brand enter new markets, add value & premium price, attract more awareness, or engender more loyalty.


I wanted to create a GPT to help companies, start-ups & students innovate more thoroughly, quickly & creatively, using the highly curated, uniquely organized Catalyzing Innovation database with its examples from many sectors globally. Catalyzing Innovation INNOVIZER® is a tool to catalyze & jump start multi-faceted product idea development in a way that’s fun to use & that will generate concept visuals & descriptions that can be evaluated by target consumers, & then iterated upon & improved.
